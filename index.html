STEVE JOBS

The Life Of Steve Jobs

Timeline

  • 1976: Apple Starts in Jobs's garage
  • 1984: Apple introduces the Macintosh
  • 1985: Jobs is ousted from Apple, founds NeXT
  • 1986: Jobs funds The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar)
  • 1996: Apple acquires NeXT
  • 1997: Jobs becomes Apple CEO
  • 1998: Apple introduces the iMac
  • 2001: Apple introduces the iPod
  • 2007: Apple releases the iPhone
  • 2010: Apple releases the iPad
  • 2011: Jobs dies of pancreatic cancer
